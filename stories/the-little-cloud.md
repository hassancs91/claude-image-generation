# The Little Cloud

High in the sky lived a little cloud named Pip. He was the smallest cloud of all.

Every day, the big clouds made rain for the fields below. Boom! Their thunder was loud and proud.

Pip wanted to make rain too. But when he tried, only one tiny drop fell. Plip!

"You are too small," rumbled the big clouds. Pip felt smaller than ever.

So Pip drifted away, down low over a quiet green meadow.

There, on the dry yellow grass, was one little flower. Its petals were drooping and sad.

Pip looked at his one tiny drop. Maybe one drop was not so small after all.

He floated close and let his little drop fall. Plip! It landed right on the thirsty flower.

The flower lifted its head and opened bright and pink. "Thank you, little cloud!" it sang.

Pip smiled the biggest smile a little cloud can smile. He was just the right size to help.
