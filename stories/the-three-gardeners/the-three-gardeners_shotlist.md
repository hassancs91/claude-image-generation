# Shot List: The Three Gardeners
**Total scenes:** 10
**Estimated cost:** ~$1.50 (10 scenes × nano-banana-pro $0.15) + $0.40 refs ≈ $1.90
**Model policy:** Smart mix → every scene has 2+ characters in frame, so all 10 use **nano-banana-pro**. References used `nano-banana-2`.

Reference plates (Fal URLs):
- mira, theo, rosa (characters) · cottage, garden (locations)

Ref order in each call: characters → location → previous scene. Cap ~5.

---

## Scene 1 — HARD CUT (cover)
- **Type/angle:** wide establishing, eye-level
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, cottage
- **Action:** Mira and little Theo run up the stone path; Grandma Rosa waves from the porch. First morning of summer, bright and warm.
- **Notes:** Establish all three + the cottage. Theo's gap-tooth + green cap visible; Mira's two red-ribbon puffs; Rosa's glasses + rose apron.

## Scene 2 — HARD CUT
- **Type/angle:** establishing (the garden), slightly high
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, garden
- **Action:** The three stand at the garden looking at drooping, thirsty flowers. Grandma's hand to her cheek, concerned.
- **Garden state:** WILTING — flowers drooping, pale dry soil, dull colours, a few fallen petals.

## Scene 3 — SOFT CUT
- **Type/angle:** reaction, eye-level
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, garden, prev=scene2
- **Action:** Mira raises a hand "we can help!", Theo nods hard (cap tilting), Grandma smiles warmly.
- **Garden state:** WILTING.

## Scene 4 — SOFT CUT
- **Type/angle:** action, eye-level
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, garden, prev=scene3
- **Action:** Grandma hands out the three watering cans — large copper (her), sky-blue (Mira), tiny yellow (Theo); the trio steps into the garden.
- **Notes:** Introduce the three exact cans. Garden still WILTING.

## Scene 5 — SOFT CUT (solo-pair)
- **Type/angle:** action, eye-level, closer
- **Refs:** mira, theo, garden, prev=scene4
- **Action:** Mira fills the cans at the faded-blue iron pump; Theo laughs as cool water splashes his bare toes.
- **Notes:** ONLY Mira and Theo in frame — Grandma Rosa is NOT present. Garden WILTING.

## Scene 6 — SOFT CUT
- **Type/angle:** action, wide
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, garden, prev=scene5
- **Action:** All three water different flowers — Grandma the tall sunflowers, Mira the pink roses, Theo the little daisies.
- **Garden state:** BEING REVIVED — soil dark and wet, colour returning.

## Scene 7 — SOFT CUT
- **Type/angle:** action/reaction, eye-level
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, garden, prev=scene6
- **Action:** Theo trips on a stone, tiny can rolling away, lip wobbling; Mira catches the can and hands it back; Grandma laughs kindly.
- **Garden state:** BEING REVIVED.

## Scene 8 — SOFT CUT (garden state change)
- **Type/angle:** establishing, wide, slightly high
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, garden, prev=scene7
- **Action:** By noon the garden is lush and happy; flowers upright and vivid, bees and butterflies; the three smile, a little smaller in the wide frame.
- **Garden state:** LUSH — upright vivid flowers, dewy, butterflies. (Describe explicitly so the cascade doesn't carry the wilting look.)

## Scene 9 — SOFT CUT (weather)
- **Type/angle:** action, eye-level
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, garden, prev=scene8
- **Action:** Soft summer rain falls; the three gardeners twirl and giggle together, faces up to the gentle rain.
- **Garden state:** LUSH, rain-fresh. Soft grey-blue rain clouds, warm light through.

## Scene 10 — HARD CUT (closing)
- **Type/angle:** reaction, cozy medium
- **Refs:** mira, theo, rosa, cottage
- **Action:** Evening on the porch — the three sit close with mugs of warm cocoa, the green-golden garden glowing behind. Soft dusk / lantern light.
- **Notes:** New location + time (porch at dusk) → no previous-scene ref. Warm closing mood.
