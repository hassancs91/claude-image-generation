# Visual Bible — The Three Gardeners

**Style sheet (verbatim in every prompt):**
> Warm, soft watercolor children's-book illustration. Gentle rounded shapes, visible paper texture, cozy golden lighting, a sunlit summer palette of greens, butter-yellows, and soft rose-pinks. Friendly, wholesome, picture-book mood.

**Universal rules (every prompt):** a purely pictorial illustration with clean, unmarked surfaces — no text, labels, signs, or readable writing anywhere. Full-bleed: the art extends to all four canvas edges, no border, frame, vignette, or paper-edge.

---

## Cast (one solo reference each — never a group portrait)

### Mira — the older sister (~7)
- Warm brown skin, round friendly face, big dark-brown eyes, a light sprinkle of freckles across the nose.
- Dark curly hair in **two round puffs**, each tied with a **small red ribbon**.
- **Short-sleeved buttercup-yellow dress with tiny white polka dots** (shoulders and torso covered), white ankle socks, simple white canvas sneakers.
- Bright, can-do expression; often mid-smile.
- **Distinguishing:** the two red ribbons + polka-dot dress. Carries the **sky-blue middle watering can**.

### Theo — the little brother (~4)
- Same warm brown skin as Mira, chubby cheeks, big gap-toothed grin, short dark curls.
- Wears a **green ball cap**, sitting slightly askew.
- **Blue-and-white horizontally striped t-shirt**, **denim overall shorts** (one strap occasionally loose), **brown leather sandals** (bare toes visible).
- Playful, giggly, a touch clumsy.
- **Distinguishing:** green askew cap + striped shirt + overalls. Carries the **tiny sunny-yellow watering can**.

### Grandma Rosa — the grandmother (~70)
- Warm light-brown skin, soft kind face with gentle laugh-lines and rosy cheeks.
- **Silver hair in a soft low bun**, **round gold wire glasses**.
- **Sage-green long-sleeved blouse**, a **cream floral apron with small rose print**, a **long denim-blue skirt**, brown garden clogs. A small **silver locket** at her neck.
- Warm, patient, twinkly-eyed.
- **Distinguishing:** silver bun + round glasses + rose-print apron + locket. Carries the **large weathered-copper watering can**.

---

## Locations

### cottage-exterior
A cozy little **stone cottage** with a **mossy-green wooden porch**, climbing pink roses around the doorway, a winding stone path through grass, warm summer sky. Used in scenes 1 and 10 (porch at dusk in scene 10 — golden lantern light).

### garden
A **walled flower garden** behind the cottage: raised wooden beds, a row of **tall sunflowers** along the back, **pink rose bushes**, a patch of **little white-and-yellow daisies**, a cobblestone path, and an **old cast-iron water pump painted faded blue** in one corner. Soft summer light.

---

## Recurring props
- **Three watering cans (always these exact ones):** large weathered-copper (Grandma), sky-blue middle (Mira), tiny sunny-yellow (Theo).
- **The blue water pump** — faded cast-iron, in the garden corner.

## Prop-state tracker — the garden (its revival is the story's arc)
| Scenes | Garden state |
|---|---|
| 1 | Seen from the path; not yet focal. |
| 2–5 | **Wilting:** flowers drooping low, dry pale soil, dull/faded colors, a few fallen petals. |
| 6–7 | **Being revived:** soil dark and wet, flowers beginning to perk up, brighter color returning. |
| 8–10 | **Lush:** flowers upright and vivid, dewy, bees and butterflies, rich greens (rain-fresh in 9). |

**Editorial (child-safe):** all three designs are modest — covered shoulders/torso, sensible everyday clothing, gentle wholesome poses. No text on any image.
