# Visual Bible: *Pepper Runs Off*

**Style (LOCKED):** Warm, soft watercolor children's-book illustration. Gentle rounded shapes, visible soft watercolor texture, cozy warm lighting, friendly faces. Slightly desaturated, inviting palette.

**Aspect ratio (LOCKED):** 4:5 portrait.

---

## Cast

### Mia — older sister
- **Age:** ~8
- **Build:** average, a little taller than Sam
- **Skin tone:** warm light brown
- **Hair:** dark brown, worn in **two braids**, each tied with a small **yellow ribbon**
- **Eyes:** warm brown
- **Distinguishing features:** the two braids + yellow ribbons; a **teal (turquoise) short-sleeved dress with small white polka dots**; white leggings; white sneakers
- **Default expression:** caring, responsible, a gentle big-sister smile
- **Consistent:** true

### Sam — younger brother
- **Age:** ~5
- **Build:** small for age, round cheeks
- **Skin tone:** warm light brown (matches Mia — siblings)
- **Hair:** short, dark, softly curly
- **Eyes:** warm brown
- **Distinguishing features:** a **mustard-yellow t-shirt with a small white star** on the chest; **blue denim dungaree shorts**; **red sneakers**
- **Default expression:** wide-eyed, excitable, easily worried
- **Consistent:** true

### Pepper — the puppy
- **Type:** small beagle-type puppy
- **Build:** tiny, round, clumsy-cute
- **Coat:** **white chest and legs with soft brown patches** over the back and head; **long floppy brown ears**
- **Eyes:** big dark friendly eyes
- **Distinguishing features:** **bright red collar** (always); long floppy ears; wagging tail
- **Default expression:** happy, eager, tongue often out
- **Consistent:** true

### The kind fruit seller — appears scenes 11–12
- **Age:** ~55
- **Build:** sturdy, warm
- **Skin tone:** warm brown
- **Hair:** grey-streaked dark hair in a **low bun**
- **Eyes:** kind, crinkly
- **Distinguishing features:** **forest-green apron** over a **cream long-sleeved blouse**; rosy cheeks; warm, reassuring smile
- **Default expression:** gentle, welcoming
- **Consistent:** true

### One-scene background characters (inline only — no reference)
- **Flower lady (scene 7):** a friendly woman at the flower cart, simple lavender dress and sun hat, arranging red roses.
- **Gray cat (scene 8):** a big fluffy gray cat perched on a wooden box at the fish stand, back slightly arched, hissing.

## Locations

### Market square — the main anchor (scenes 1–6, 10)
- **Type:** sunny open-air street market
- **Time:** bright daytime throughout
- **Key features:** cobblestone ground; rows of wooden stalls with **red-and-white and yellow striped awnings**; strings of small plain **bunting flags overhead (no text)**; wooden crates and woven baskets; a few leafy green trees; soft blue sky with a couple of fluffy clouds; a gentle crowd of friendly townsfolk
- **Mood:** lively, warm, safe, bustling

### Fruit stall (scenes 11–12)
- **Type:** a corner stall within the same market
- **Key features:** a wooden stall heaped with **colorful fruit in woven baskets** (red apples, oranges, yellow bananas, green grapes); a striped awning matching the market; warm and inviting
- **Mood:** cozy, relieved, kind

### Cozy bedroom at night — inline (scene 13 closing)
- **Type:** a small, snug children's bedroom
- **Key features:** two low beds with soft quilts (one teal-ish, one yellow-ish to echo the kids); a **round window with a few stars and a crescent moon**; a small warm bedside lamp glow; a soft rug
- **Mood:** peaceful, warm, sleepy
- *(single appearance — described inline in the scene prompt, no reference plate)*
